VR Technology

As the title implies, our project will be focusing on the progression of VR Technology, from its first inception to present day. This includes the various products that utilize VR, the development processes behind these products, and the significance of the creation and spread of this technology. We will also touch upon some of the more popular examples such as the Oculus Rift and the Meta Quest 2 to see what this technology could mean for the future. The portal will predominantly feature the applications VR has and how they could be extended to other uses in the future, such as product demonstrations and virtual tours.
